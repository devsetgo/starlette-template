About

As a fan of music, a drummer, and a developer. I decided to create a Twitter bot inspried by Africa by Toto Bot. I found this account intersting and enjoyed watching the interaction that others had with the bot. Personally, I just had fun commenting on the various lines as if they were said to me direct.

I started thinking about how various songs touched me and brought back fond memories. So I created my first twitter bot for Like a Rock by Bob Seger and hope to create others overtime for others to enjoy and remember great music.
